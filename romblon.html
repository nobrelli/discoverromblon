About Romblon

The Marblous Province of the Philippines

Discover The Hidden World

If you have ever had traveled to Mindoro, Marinduque, or Palawan, you might also be interested to travel in Romblon. It is one of the beautiful provinces of the MIMAROPA region of the Philippines you will have ever been at. On a map's scale, you'll see that Romblon is just a small group of islands, yet it offers travelers a lot of Instagramable scenic geography. You just have to travel 360 around its three major and four minor islands so as for you to get the idea on how you will get the most of your trip.

Romblon brings back pieces of memories from the history, although it hasn't quite been at the height of its fame since it is secluded from the larger islands of the Philippine archipelago, and some tourists have not yet reached the very peak of its beauty ever since they had visited the place. Due to the isolation, the untouched and pristine blue beaches blessed this place; therefore, Romblon has been a traveler's paradise and a place for people who feel lost and look for a home.

The Romblon’s Vistas

Traveling around Romblon is a new stunning adventure wherever you go around. It has countless features to offer, despite being just a small landmass. Every inch screams the word "beautiful," from the extraordinary beaches to the breathtaking falls and the people who live in it. One notable example of these is an extraordinary place called Bonbon Beach located in Barangay Lonos. Bonbon is popularly known for its long stretch of pristine white sand beach and a sandbar that connects it to Bang-og Island during low tide. It is privately owned, and you will not see any artificial structures around. However, only a few tourists visit this place unlike Boracay, Palawan, etc.; therefore, you have the freedom to enjoy the picturesque and magnificent view of this place during off seasons. This beach is perfect for people who were already fed up with the aura of some overcrowded beaches. You'll just have to take that #PerfectSelfie with the view.

If they're from tip to toe jaded of other people's presence and just want to look for a more relaxing, more serene and more secluded place, there's an islet on Sibuyan Island called Cresta De Gallo. It is an island surrounded by pristine blue water and is one of the most beautiful places you could ever find in Romblon. Getting there has never been as easy as you’re gonna have to traverse rough and rocky roads while taking a crack at withstanding the roasting heat of the sun. Once you have arrived on the islet, it’s all definitely worth the hike, and it would be a great challenge for you to start off! You'll be in awe of how beautiful this place has always been.

They are not just the beautiful pristine blue beaches that you'll see in Romblon. This place is also home for numerous majestic waterfalls. Each has their own distinct features. To name a few, Dulom Falls, also known as the "Dark Falls," as only few sunlight pass through; the Mablaran Falls, an epic and most popular waterfalls in San Andres; and Garing Falls in Odiongan, Romblon, which is a two-layered falls with a pool below, which is perfect for swimming and diving.

It won’t be complete without some knickknacks

As your trip to this wonderful place ends, it wouldn't be complete if you would hesitate to visit their famous Marble Shopping Center at Brgy. Poclacion, Romblon, Romblon. After all, it is the Marble Capital of The Philippines. You can buy great quality marble souvenirs in different sizes at a very affordable price. There's also a rare and expensive type of marble, the Romblon black marble (Black Onyx), which is perfect for some collectors. Also, the century-old marbles are handcrafted by the talented people of Romblon.

Wrapping things up

Traveling around Romblon would be an unforgettable moment! Besides its untouched natural features, the folks are very hospitable, considerate, and loyal to one another, so you won't start to feel yourself alone or being just an outcast when you visit or plan to visit the place.

"It's more fun in the Philippines," has always been a mantra of most local and foreign travelers when they visit the country, but specifically it's more fun in Romblon as it offers more relaxing and more exquisite view of nature from soothing pristine beaches to overlooking mountaintops. Living in Romblon is a fresh breeze than living in a honking city.

Are you now craving this place? Then add Romblon to you travel list now! It will be a great advantage for you to start planning out your journey to the Marblous Province of the Philippines!
